## The Story

There's something magical about the first light of day. The world is quiet, the air is crisp, and the landscape transforms through a spectrum of colors that disappear within minutes. This collection represents three years of early mornings across the American West, from lakeshores in the Upper Midwest to canyon walls in Utah.

Each photograph tells a story of patience and timing—waiting for the perfect moment when light, weather, and composition align.

### The Process

Landscape photography requires careful planning and often a bit of luck. I scout locations during the day, studying how the terrain interacts with light. Then I return before sunrise, setting up in darkness and waiting for that brief window when everything comes together.

**Equipment used:**

- Canon EOS 5D Mark III & Sony α7R III
- Wide-angle lenses (14-24mm, 24-70mm)
- Sturdy tripod for long exposures
- Graduated ND filters for balanced exposure

### Technical Approach

These images were captured using a combination of techniques:

- **Long exposures** to smooth water and capture cloud movement
- **Focus stacking** for maximum depth of field in canyon shots
- **Graduated filters** to balance bright skies with darker foregrounds
- **Careful post-processing** to enhance natural colors without oversaturation

## Featured Images

The collection includes three signature pieces showcasing different aspects of morning light:

**Calm Morning** — Upper Midwest lakefront at sunrise. The absolute stillness of the water created a perfect mirror, doubling the pastel sky and creating a sense of infinite peace.

**Morning Colors** — Zion National Park in autumn. Cottonwood trees against red canyon walls, illuminated by the warm glow of early morning light. This was taken during a brief two-week window when the leaves were perfectly gold.

**Morning Storm** — Dramatic storm clouds at sunrise. Sometimes the most compelling images come from unpredictable weather. I was packed up and ready to leave when a break in the clouds created this spectacular scene.
